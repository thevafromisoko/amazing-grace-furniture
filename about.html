Our Story

From humble beginnings in Ozoro, Delta State, Amazing Grace Furniture was founded with a vision: to craft furniture that blends local heritage with modern style. What started as a family workshop has grown into a full-service design and furniture company known across Delta for quality, honesty, and elegance.

Our Core Values

  • Craftsmanship: Every piece is built with care, by hand.
  • Community: We invest in local artisans and materials.
  • Trust: We deliver what we promise, always.
