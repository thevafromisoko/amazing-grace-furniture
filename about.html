Our Story

Founded in Ozoro, Delta State, Amazing Grace Furniture has grown from a humble workshop to a respected name in handcrafted furniture. Our passion lies in designing custom pieces that reflect both tradition and innovation.

Where It All Began

Started in a small workshop in Ozoro, our founder believed in the power of locally made, quality furniture. Over the years, we've grown — but our commitment to hand-finished excellence remains.
